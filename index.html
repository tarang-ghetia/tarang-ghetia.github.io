05 Experience

Where I've worked

  1. Research Assistant (HiWi)

    Oct 2024 – Present

    RWTH Aachen · ANTS — applied ML for multi-modal spectroscopy & industrial material sorting

    • Build classification pipelines that sort 7+ materials — plus finer composition-based sub-classes — from NIR, MIR, FIR and hyperspectral data: 0.91 F1 on live industrial sorting, with material properties read straight from the spectra.
    • Recorded the datasets from scratch: millions of spectra captured across varying conditions and hand-annotated, since nothing off-the-shelf fit these materials.
    • Wrote the team's internal tooling — a custom annotation app that cut labeling time, and a graph-generation tool whose plots go into the group's papers.
    • Prototype in the lab; once it proves out, it moves onto the client's production line.

  2. Deep-Learning Research Intern

    Jan – Mar 2024

    Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)

    • Semantic segmentation of Landsat-8 imagery with U-Net, SegFormer and Barlow-Twin U-Net on the 38-Cloud dataset (18,000+ patches) — 97.04% accuracy, holding up even on thin clouds and haze.
    • Self-supervised pretraining with Barlow Twins and transformer backbones — Dice 0.8944, recall pushed to 93.28% (BT-UNet).

  3. Research Assistant

    Aug 2022 – Dec 2024

    Pandit Deendayal Energy University

    • Led ML projects on environmental forecasting (PM2.5 / PM10), crop and soil quality, and groundwater contamination — LSTM, GRU, transformers and VAR on 18,000+ data points.
    • Backed the forecasts with proper statistics: Granger causality, Johansen cointegration and ADF tests.

  4. Full-Stack Developer Intern

    May – Jul 2023

    Unitech Technocrats Pvt. Ltd.

    • Built and maintained a Flask web application, improved the enterprise frontend and hardened authentication through custom API integrations.
